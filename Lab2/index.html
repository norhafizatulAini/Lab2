About

Nims is produced by Crispy Choco Jar Adeliciousz and its founder is Tengku Norhanim. Nims Crispy Choco Tub is the first chocolate product innovation in Malaysia in 2014. Previously known as Crispy Choco Jar which is packaged using a glass jar and before eating must be soaked in hot water first to melt the chocolate. Once this product was released, many brands have released more or less similar products. So, in 2019 Nims has upgraded its chocolate products into tubs that are Ready to Eat and every purchase will come with a spoon. Crispy Choco Tub does not need to be soaked in hot water before it is eaten as it is a Ready Eat product. Nims Choco Tub has released several types such as coco ball, coco rice, coco crisp, mini coco crunch, rainbow cereal, and crispy choco cluster.
